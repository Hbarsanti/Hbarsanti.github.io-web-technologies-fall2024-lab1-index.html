All about Hockey

The Game

Hockey, or more specifically Ice Hockey, is a fast pace 6v6 game with 5 players and one goalie on each team. Each player is on skates and has a stick and the goal is to put the puck in the opponents net.

“Hockey is a unique sport in the sense that you need each and every guy helping each other and pulling in the same direction to be successfull.” - Wayne Gretzky

Why people play hockey

There are many reasons why Hockey is loved worldwide:

  • It not only helps people improve physical skills but also mental and emotional skills.
  • It is fast paced and unlike any other sport.
